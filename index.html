Objectives of the Mission

Pollution Reduction

Reduce industrial and sewage pollution in the river by implementing advanced treatment plants and monitoring industrial waste disposal.

Learn More

River Basin Management

Ensure sustainable management of the Ganga basin by regulating water use, preventing over-extraction, and maintaining ecological balance.

Learn More

Riverfront Development

Develop riverfronts with walkways, parks, and ghats to promote tourism while maintaining cleanliness and environmental sustainability.

Learn More

Afforestation

Plant trees along the riverbanks to prevent erosion, improve air quality, and enhance the overall biodiversity of the river ecosystem.

Learn More

Public Awareness

Increase awareness among communities, educate citizens about river conservation, and encourage active participation in cleanup drives.

Learn More

Livelihood Development

Promote sustainable livelihoods for communities dependent on the river by supporting eco-friendly businesses and responsible fishing practices.

Learn More

Patna
